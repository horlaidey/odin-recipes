Ingredients

  • Yam
  • Seasoning
  • Bell pepper
  • Tomato Paste
  • Chicken
  • Palm Oil
  • Salt et al

Steps

  1. Grind the bell pepper
  2. Boil the yam a bit
  3. Pour the half-boiled yam into a basket
  4. fry grinded pepper and tomato paste with the palm oil
  5. add seasoning and all sort
  6. pour the half-boiled yam and stir till it all get muchy
  7. leave to steam for a while
